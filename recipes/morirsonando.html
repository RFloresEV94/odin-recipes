Morir Soñando

A creamy and smooth orange refresher

Enjoy this sweet and tasty drink made with fresh squeezed orange juice and milk
and transport yourself to a Dominican beach enjoying the sunset while sipping on
this wonderful treat!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups evaporated milk
  • ¾ cup sugar (white, granulated)
  • 1 tablespoon clear vanilla extract (otional)
  • 2 cups of ice cubes
  • 2 ½ cup orange juice

Instructions

  1. Chill milk. Add sugar to milk to taste. Stir in vanilla. Add ice and stir.
    Pour in the orange juice and stir.
  2. Serve immediately.

Tip

The trick for the milk not to curdle is keeping the milk (and the whole drink afterward) at a very low temperature.
